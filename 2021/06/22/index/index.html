Biography

I obtained my Master's degree in Signal and Information Processing from Anhui University in June 2007 and completed my Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology under the guidance of Professor Luo Bin at the same institution from September 2011 to December 2015. During my Ph.D., I had the opportunity to be a joint-trained doctoral student at the University of Stirling, UK, in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science under the supervision of Professor Amir Hussain from July 2013 to July 2014. Now I'm a full associate professor at Anhui University. My research interests are Multimodal Cognitive Computing, Medical Image Analysis (Ultrasound, Terahertz), Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence.
